Our Story

Welcome to The Magic Bean, where our passion for great coffee and community comes together! Founded in 1888 by Luna Lovegood, we're dedicated to serving small-batch, expertly crafted coffee sourced from local roasters and sustainable farms. Our cozy shop is designed to feel like home, with comfortable seating, natural light, and eclectic decor, making it the perfect spot to catch up with friends, work, or enjoy some quiet time. We're more than just a coffee shop - we're a community hub, hosting regular events, live music, and art exhibits that showcase local talent. Join us and taste the difference that passion and community make in every cup!

About Us

Step into The Wizard's Brew, where magic meets coffee. Our cozy shop is inspired by Hogwarts, with freshly brewed coffee and warmth of friendship. Whether you're Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff, or Slytherin, our doors are open. Experience the wizarding world, with aromas, flavors, and community. Come, relax, and let the magic begin.

Contact Us

Lost in Diagon Alley? Need coffee guidance? Reach out to The Wizard's Brew! Send an owl, give us a call, or apparate over. We'll guide you through our wizarding coffee world. From Butterbeer Lattes to Hogwarts House Blends, we're here to help. Contact us, and let's conjure up a magical experience.

Opening Hours

The wizarding world needs coffee! Our hours are crafted for your magical needs. From morning Butterbeer Lattes to late-night study sessions, we're here. Early risers, night owls, and everyone in between – come, relax, and recharge. Our doors are open, and our coffee is brewing. Visit us, and let the magic of The Wizard's Brew begin.
